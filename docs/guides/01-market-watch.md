# 01. Market Watch & Real-time Tracking

**Dashboard** is the market observation station for CryptoFolio. It is designed to provide you with the most up-to-date price dynamics of your selected assets.

## Real-time Price Board (Pinned Pairs)

In the center of the homepage, you can check all "pinned" trading pairs at a glance:
- **Real-time Sync**: Every 5 minutes (or when you manually pull to refresh), the system automatically updates the latest transaction price via the Binance API.
- **Status Indicator**: A blinking green dot indicates that the currency is currently connected in the system and has a corresponding active position.
- **Quick Jump**: Click any price card to quickly focus on the transaction history of that asset.

## Refresh Mechanism

- **Automatic Update**: When you stay on the Dashboard page, the prices of assets involved in active positions will be automatically polled in the background.
- **Pull-to-Refresh**: Pull down the page on mobile or desktop to force a full price update and synchronize global market conditions instantly.
