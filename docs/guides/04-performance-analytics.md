# 04. Performance & PnL Analytics (Performance)

At the top of the **Positions** page, CryptoFolio presents you with a set of health reports for your entire investment system.

## Core Indicator Analysis

1. **Realized PnL**:
   - Reflects the net profit generated by all **Closed** or partially closed Strategic positions in history.
2. **Unrealized PnL**:
   - Calculates the book value of current active positions based on real-time current prices.
3. **Win Rate**:
   - `Profitable positions / Total closed positions`.
4. **Global ROI**:
   - `(Total Profit / Cumulative Investment Capital) * 100%`. Measures your growth efficiency per unit of capital.

## Global Time Range Filter

The filter menu in the upper right corner (`1M`, `3M`, `All Time`) has high priority:
- **Dynamic Recalculation**: After switching the time range, all summary cards will **only calculate positions that closed or were active during that time**.
- **Trend Analysis**: Use this function to quickly compare changes in operation success rates between last month and this quarter.

---

> [!NOTE]
> **Regarding Data Accuracy**
> 
> The system internally uses a high-precision Math engine to minimize the small errors caused by JS floating-point calculations, ensuring alignment with exchange accounts.
